Plain English Summary
This is a 3-year office lease for 2,500 sq ft at $4,200/month. The landlord can increase rent by up to 5% annually. You're responsible for all maintenance over $500. Early termination requires 6 months' rent as penalty.
✅ Favorable Terms
60-day grace period for late payments
Landlord covers structural repairs
Subletting allowed with approval
Free parking (5 spots included)
⚠️ Unfavorable Terms
5% annual rent increase (market avg: 3%)
6-month penalty for early termination
90-day non-renewal notice (easy to miss)
Landlord can relocate you within building
🔓 Vulnerabilities
HIGH Section 12.3: Landlord can terminate with 30-day notice "for any business reason"
MED Section 8.1: Vague "normal wear and tear" definition — deposit dispute risk
LOW Section 15.2: Arbitration clause limits ability to sue
