Differentiation

The gap nobody fills

Every competitor either costs too much, does too little, or treats AI as an afterthought. Including LegalAI — our closest competitor.

Too Expensive

Harvey ($1,200/mo), CoCounsel ($225-500/mo), Legora (€1-3K/yr), Luminance (custom enterprise) — all priced for BigLaw budgets, not solo practitioners earning $70-120K.

Too Narrow

Spellbook = contracts only. EvenUp = PI only. CaseMark = summarization only. No single platform combines core tasks a solo lawyer needs daily.

AI as Afterthought

Clio and Smokeball are practice management first — AI is a bolt-on feature, not the core. Smokeball just partnered with CoCounsel instead of building own AI.

LegalAI.com — Closest Rival

Same target (solo/small firms), $39-179/mo, 9 tools. But: breadth over depth, no practice area specialization, no adaptive learning, generic output — a "checkbox AI" that does many things poorly.
